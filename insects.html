Beetle

Beetles are found in almost every habitat except the sea and the polar regions, they interact with their ecosystems in several ways: beetles often feed on plants and fungi, break down animal and plant debris, and eat other invertebrates.

Orthoptera

Several species of Orthoptera are considered pests of crops and rangelands or seeking warmth in homes by humans.

Additional Info
Lacewings

Lacewings occur in field and tree crops, gardens and landscapes, and wildlands. Adults feed on honeydew, plant nectar, and yeasts.
